2.1 Vectorization

An nice feature of is its vectorized capabilities. Take for example two vectors: \[ \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \\ 6 \end{bmatrix} \] code:

a <- c(1,2,3)
b <- c(1,2,3)
a+b
## [1] 2 4 6
